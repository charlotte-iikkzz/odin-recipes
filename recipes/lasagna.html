Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown ground beef with garlic and onion in a skillet.
  4. Mix tomato sauce with the cooked beef.
  5. Layer noodles, beef mixture, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until bubbly.
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
